My Biography

Husayn was born into the linage of Abdulrazaq Alawiye. Being the second from a family of three. He hails from the creeks of lagos Island - A community filled with dynamic and resilient minds. Husayn had his primary education from Our-Lady of Apostles, yaba, Lagos and completd his secondary education in Federal Science and technical College, Ilesa, Osun State.



Family Background

He was born into the family of Abdul-razak Alawiye (A down-to-earth secondary school teacher) and Riskiat Jimoh (A self-made seamstress) and teacher. He is the second of a twin (Kehinde) popularly referred to as "Ibeji" in yoruba land. He is followed by two younger siblings.



Work Life

Husayn started his career as a customer service representative in a financial institution in Nigeria for 2 years, where he later moved into the fintech space as a social media specialist for about 6 months. He was promoted to a senior role and subsequently handled training for new hires.
